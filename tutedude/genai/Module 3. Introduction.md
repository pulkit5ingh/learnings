# What is GEN AI ?

- Gen ai is type of artificial intelligence that creates new content such as texts, music or code by learning patterns from existing data and is capable of mimicking human creativity.
- **Generative AI (Gen AI)** is a type of Artificial Intelligence that can **create new content** such as:

* Text (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini)
* Images (DALL·E, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion)
* Code (GitHub Copilot, Cursor AI)
* Audio and Music
* Videos
* Documents and Reports

Unlike traditional AI, which mainly analyzes or classifies data, Gen AI **generates something new** based on the data it has learned from.

### Example

**Generative AI**

### How Gen AI Works

Most modern Gen AI systems use **Large Language Models (LLMs)** such as:

- OpenAI GPT models
- Anthropic Claude
- Google Gemini
- Meta Llama
- DeepSeek

These models are trained on massive amounts of data and learn patterns in language, code, images, and more.

4. Learn Prompt Engineering.
5. Learn RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation).

8. Build 5–10 real-world projects.
9. Learn deployment and monitoring.

# Senior Backend/Fullstack Developer – Microspace

**What they want (point by point)**

---

## Role focus

- Senior developer who **owns outcomes** and understands how technical choices affect product and customers.
- **Backend-first**, but comfortable across the stack (backend + some frontend).
- Work on the **core of the platform** for a SaaS used by customer-facing teams (branded digital spaces, collaboration, engagement).

---

## What you will do

- **Own** core backend building blocks and services; evolve them as product and integrations change.
- Build and evolve core backend with a **long-term product mindset**.
- **Design and maintain** APIs, data models, and integrations, including:
  - Authentication and SSO flows
  - External integrations
  - Backend-driven views
- Work **across service boundaries** and understand full request and data flows.
- **Collaborate** with frontend on end-to-end features.
- **Contribute** to architectural decisions and technical direction.
- **Improve** reliability, performance, and operational robustness.
- Work with **AWS and DevOps** to keep the platform observable, scalable, and reliable.
- **Participate** in planning, estimation, and prioritisation of product work.
- **Own code quality**: peer reviews, test automation, secure coding.

---

## What they are looking for (requirements)

- **Senior** developer with ownership mindset.
- **B2B SaaS** experience with complex workflows and user roles.
- **Backend**: strong Node.js and TypeScript; experience with a structured framework (e.g. **NestJS**).
- **PostgreSQL**: schema design and database migrations.
- **Frontend**: solid understanding of frontend architecture, ideally **React**.
- **AWS**: experience in AWS-based environments.
- **Auth**: experience with authentication, authorisation, **SSO** concepts and integrations.
- **Product-aware**: can question and refine requirements.
- **Team**: comfortable in a small, distributed team.
- **Communication**: clear English.

---

## What they offer

- Work in a **growing SaaS startup** used by well-known Dutch brands.
- **Direct influence** on architecture, integrations, and product direction.
- **Small team**, short lines, high ownership.
- **Long-term product work** (not project-based delivery).

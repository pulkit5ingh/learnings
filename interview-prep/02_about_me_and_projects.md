## Interview prep tips

- **Microspace fit:** Emphasise NestJS, TypeScript, PostgreSQL (mention migrations and schema design where you’ve done it), AWS, auth/SSO-style work (RBAC, JWT, integrations), B2B SaaS (Tripinnov, Ordercoro, Robofood, ShiftPro), and working with frontend/product (Fliqa, multi-role products).
- **Story format:** For each project: problem → your role → tech choices → outcome or lesson.
- **Depth:** Pick 2–3 projects you can go deep on (e.g. TravelBaba.ai for AI + NestJS, ShiftPro for marketplace, Corpusvision for RBAC and payments).

**Type:** Assignment

---

## Goal

---

- Bar chart (5 images)
- Line chart (5 images)
- Pie chart (5 images)
- Scatter plot (3 images)
- Combined/complex chart (2 images)

Sources: download from financial reports, Wikipedia, research papers, or generate with matplotlib.
